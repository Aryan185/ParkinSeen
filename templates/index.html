About Parkinson's Disease

Parkinson's disease is a progressive nervous system disorder that affects movement. Symptoms start gradually, sometimes with a barely noticeable tremor in just one hand. Tremors are common, but the disorder also commonly causes stiffness or slowing of movement.

How to Perform the Test

To perform the video test, click on the "Take Video Test" button below. You'll be directed to a page where you'll be asked to record a video of yourself performing certain movements. Follow the instructions carefully to ensure accurate results.

To perform the audio test, click on the "Take Audio Test" button below. You'll be directed to a page where you'll be asked to record an audio of yourself saying certain syllables. Follow the instructions carefully to ensure accurate results.
